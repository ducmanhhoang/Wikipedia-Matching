0Born in Piacenza, Giuseppe Merosi was a renowned automotive engineer Italian.
1After the end of his technical studies and his diploma of land surveyor-expert and his military service, he created in 1893 a small construction company bicycles, which stayed in business until 1898, when he was recruited by the company "Orio & Marchand", an important company of Piacenza, for which it will design cars and cycles motorized until the summer of 1904.
2He is then recruited by the design office of FIAT, where he will participate actively in the design of several competition cars.
3At the end of the year 1905, he moved to Milan, where he is recruited by the company "Lentz" to design a car that will be built in three copies.
4In the fall of 1906 he was recruited by the company Bianchi, where he worked until September 1909.
5In 1910, he was recruited by the Italian car maker Alfa Romeo and was appointed technical director and head of the project department of the new Factory of Alfa Romeo, of Portello in Milan.
6The first car he designed was the ALFA 24 HP.
7Editor's note : at this time, the name that Alfa Romeo did not yet exist not, it was simply A. L. F. A. - Anonima Lombarda Automobili.
8Nicola Romeo will not change the name ALFA Alfa Romeo that, in August, 1915.
9The ALFA 24 HP was presented on June 24, 1910.
10It had an engine designed by G. Merosi with a capacity of 4.084 cm3 developing an extraordinary power of 42 Hp, which allowed him to reach without difficulty the terrific speed of 100 km/h.
11The car was a huge success, in the first year alone it sold 50 copies.
12In 1914, G. Merosi designed the first engine with a distribution system DOHC-dual overhead cam, 16-valve, 8-valve, intake and 8 exhaust, on an engine with 4 cylinders to 4.500 cm3 of cubic capacity.
13this engine was installed in a competition car.The activity of G. Merosi continued during the first world war with the realization of generators and compressors for military uses.
14At the request of Nicola Romeo, he was appointed head of the company "Officine Ferroviarie Meridionali during the period 1917-1918, but it will resume as early as 1919 his activity as an engineer designer engineer civil, which was become the one who is still living of these days : Alfa Romeo.
15It is different with new engines with two overhead camshafts.
16At the beginning of the 1920s, it will present the luxurious sedan Alfa Romeo G1.Merosi has always attached great importance to motor racing, so the races to ensure the development of the company and assert its image.
17In 1911, ALFA took part for the first time at the Targa Florio with ALFA 24HP.
18the first victory of this race to the trademark shall be acquired in 1923.
19The following cars, real race cars were the Alfa RL, Alfa RM and Alfa P1.In the month of April, 1926, Giuseppe Merosi left the company Alfa Romeo when Vittorio Jano will replace it.
20It will work for other car manufacturers such as Isotta Fraschini.
21It will make its return to Alfa Romeo during the second world war from 1941 to 1943.
22He died at the age of 84 in Lecco.
