4Technologythe S. 13 was a single-engine biplane fighter reconnaissance flying boat with a crew of two.
5The Pilot and observer sat side by side in the open Cockpit, and were protected only by a windshield.
6The canoe-shaped hull consisted of three layers of wood.
7The wings were covered, as in the previous machines SIAI S. 8 to SIAI S. 12, with linen, and with spars and steel wires attached to the fuselage.
8The S. 13 was powered by a Isotta Fraschini V6 engine with 250 horsepower and a two-blade propeller.
9The armament consisted of a 7.7 mm machine gun.
