0Design and développementLe S. 19 is a seaplane, single seat, biplane with an engine mounted on struts between the hull and the upper wing.
1The engine Ansaldo San Giorgio 4E-29L'Aviation: RAIS seaplane race (Race hydroplanes RAIS) 500 hp is equipped with a pusher propeller bladed.
2Small floats of stabilization are mounted under the lower wing.
3History opérationnelleLe engine of the S. 19 was not ready in time for the Trophy Schneider 1920, the aircraft could not, therefore, not participate in it.
