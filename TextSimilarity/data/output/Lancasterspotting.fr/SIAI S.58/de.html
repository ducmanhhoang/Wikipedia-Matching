0GeschichteIm 1923, the Regia Marina launched a call for tender for a new hunting flying boat in the Italian Navy, the successor to the SIAI S. 13.
1SIAI was involved with the s. 58 in the tender, but lost out in 1929, just under the Macchi M. 41bis.
2To be able to participate in the tender of the Navy to participate, to put SIAI with the p. 58 a new project.
3The designer of the SIAI introduced in 1924, his concept in the Navy, he focused strongly on the S. 51, and added new developments, p. 56 and p. 57.
4It should be an agile, quick and stable fighter plane.
5He sat on the Hispano-Suiza 42 engine with 300 horsepower and the tried-and-tested two-blade propeller.
6The single-seat flying boat should be equipped with two 7.7 mm machine guns.
725.
8August 1924 launched the S. 58 for the first time and set at a loading of 250 kg, the rate of climb 6.5 m/s, a new world record for flying boats.
9However, none of the aircraft involved in the tender was grown, the requirements of the Navy.
10In the case of a new tender in 1927 SIAI involved with the s. 58 in the Version of S. 58bis, upgraded the machine, however, with a Fiat A 20 with 420 HP to throw more power into the equation.
11As armament, they chose two 7.7 mm Vickers machine guns.
12With repairs, it was involved in 1929, at the third invitation to tender for the Navy with the slightly improved Version of S. 58ter and was subject to just the Macchi M. 41bis.
13It was agreed, however, that the us Navy purchased the prototype for training with the marine aviators.
14The S. 58 was a double-Decker flying boat with a flat bottom wooden hull was reinforced with metal.
15She had an open Cockpit in which a Pilot could sit.
16The Fiat A-20 engine with 420 HP was fixed, as is the case with the predecessors, in the middle of the upper wing.
17Worthy of mention is the V-Shaped tail, the reinforced construction of the wings and ailerons on the upper wing, that had been installed in the p. 56 and p. 57 with success.
18The machine guns were mounted rigidly in the aircraft fuselage forward of the Cockpit.
19The first Version was equipped with a Hispano-Suiza 42 engine with 300 HP.
20In the second tender with the competition, developed an improved Version with a Fiat A20, 12-cylinder V-engine with 420 HP, which they called the S. 58bis.
21In the third tender, in 1929, in addition, metal reinforcements were made to the fuselage and on the wings, and the Pilot got a headrest; this military version was conducted under S. 58ter.
