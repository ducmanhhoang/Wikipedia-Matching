0Design and developmentThe Societa Idrovolanti Alta Italia (SIAI) was formed in 1915 initially to build French flying-boats under license.
1The company employed Rafaele Conflenti as Chief Designer and his first design was the SIAI S.8 a two-seat equal-span biplane flying boat first flown in 1917.
2The Royal Italian Navy ordered 800 S.8s to be built by SIAI and sub-contractors.
3With the end of the war production was stopped when 172 aircraft had been delivered.
4Early aircraft were powered by an Isotta Fraschini V4B engine, later aircraft had the more powerful 220 hp (164 kW) Hispano-Suiza 44.
