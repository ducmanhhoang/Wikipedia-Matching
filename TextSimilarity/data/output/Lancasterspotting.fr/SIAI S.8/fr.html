0Design and développementLa Societa Idrovolanti Alta Italia (RAIS) was established in 1915, initially to build seaplanes French under license.
1The company employed Rafaele Conflenti as head of the design office, its first realization was the RAIS S. 8, a seaplane biplane two-seater that volât for the first time in 1917.
2The Italian Navy ordered 800 S. 8 which was to be built by RAIS and sub-contractors.
3With the end of the war, the production was halted, while 172 aircraft had been delivered.
4The first examples were equipped with engines Isotta Fraschini V4B, the following had a Hispano-Suiza 44 of 220 hp.
