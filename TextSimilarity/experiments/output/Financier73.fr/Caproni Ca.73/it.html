Article: Caproni Ca.73, it

No.Content
0The Caproni Ca.73 it was a biplane, bombing light, originally conceived to operate as a civilian transport for 10 passengers with 2 crew.
1The project, developed between 1922 and 1924 by the engineer Rodolfo Verduzio, with the collaboration among others of Umberto Nobile, The configuration of the aircraft was that of a sesquiplano reversed, in which the upper wing of 18 m length was free of ailerons; the tail had configuration biplane.
2The 2 engines in-line cylinders were installed in tandem in a nacelle (without enclosure) is placed between the two wings.The aircraft was used by the wings of the Regia Aeronautica until 1934, also participating in the operations against the liberation of africa.
