0The SIAI S. 16 is a 1919 by SIAI-Marchetti developed a passenger flying boat for five persons.
1The machine has been crowned the most successful flying boat of the decade.
2In addition to several civilian versions developed SIAI also a military Version of the S. 16, and sold these in the whole world, where you are today.
3The SIAI S. 16 was a single engine flying boat that was powered by a C. D. Lorraine Dietrich 12Db engine with 400 HP, which has also been used in other aircraft successfully.
4The fuselage consisted of 30 mm thick wood veneer, the Rest of the aircraft was made of 4 mm thick wood.
5The wings were covered with fabric, wherein the front edge of the wing was reinforced.
6This design proved to be very solid.
7The machine was produced in series and exported all over the world.Already in 1920, allowed for technical innovations, improvements to the S. 16, the should be called from there, starting on p. 16.
8A new Motor from Fiat, called Fiat 12bis, with the 300 HP had less power, but received a new larger air screw, which should compensate for this.
9In the case of the S. 16bis of the fuel, improved fuel tank, as well as the hull stability.
10The military variant of the S. 16 was called S. 16bis M.
11This had a modified fuselage to be able to a MG in the bow, as well as two pylons to mount bombs on the wings.
12The military Version was sold to Spain, Brazil, Italy and the Soviet Union.
13Spain from the S. 16bis M in license and equipped with a Hispano-Suiza 300-HP engine.
