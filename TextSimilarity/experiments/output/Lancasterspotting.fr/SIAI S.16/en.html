0The SIAI S.16 was an Italian passenger flying-boat later used a military reconnaissance-bomber, claimed to be the most successful flying-boat of the 1920s.
1Design and developmentThe first flying-boat designed by the Societa Idrovolanti Alta Italia (SIAI) for use as a civilian passenger carrier, the S.13 was a biplane flying-boat with room for five passengers.
2The S.16 was powered by a single Fiat A.12bis engine.
3Military versions were also developed with a bow cockpit for an observer-gunner and bomb-racks fitted underneath the wings.
4The military version was sold to Brazil, Soviet Union, Spain and Turkey.
5Military version of the S.16bis with bow cockpit, Soviet operated versions (80 aircraft) were designated by them the S-16bis, some were licence-built in Spain.
