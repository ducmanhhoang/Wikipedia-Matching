0GeschichteDas the previous model SIAI S. 17 should win according to the plans of SIAI, the Schneider Trophy, however, was not successful, and crashed into the Lago Maggiore.
1With the p. 19 a new, more robust machine was developed that built on the conception of the p. 17.
2Like the previous models, the S. 19 was a double-deck flying boat with canoe-shaped wooden hull.
3The wings were, as with the predecessor machine SIAI S. 8 to SIAI S. 17, with linen covered with bars and steel wires attached to the fuselage.
4Since the Motor of S. 17 was too weak, was built for the S. 19 of the larger Ansaldo 4E29 V12 with 550 HP and a two-blade propeller.
5Instead of the previous engine mounting with wood spars Stahlholme were used.
6It was not, however, the engine for the Schneider Trophy in 1920 and safe to install, so the s. 19 could not participate in the race.
