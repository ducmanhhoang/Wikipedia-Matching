0The Savoia-Marchetti S. 56 is a flying boat from the Italian manufacturer Savoia-Marchetti.
1With the p. 56 realized SIAI the need for a smaller, double-deck flying boat for tourism and Trainer in the 1920s.
2The aircraft was produced, not only in series but also in other countries.
3GeschichteMit of the new p. 56 has found SIAI a gap in the market in the flying boat industry, and a further success achieved.
4The S. 56 was made of wood, had the opportunity to be with a wheel undercarriage fitted and was relatively inexpensive to purchase.
5The machine had an open Cockpit and could be controlled by the pilot, the copilot, and was, therefore, as a Trainer for pilot training.
6In addition to the two pilots, sitting side by side in the pilot cockpit, could be taken behind the pilot or a passenger.
7The Savoia-Marchetti S. 56 was equipped with a 70 HP radial engine of Anzani and a wooden two-blade propeller.
8With an improved Motor from the Anzani with 80 HP SIAI participated in the regular air race "Sea Cup" in Naples, however, to win without a prize in the class.
9Later wish, were installed on the customer's other engines of the Fiat A53 115 PS, Fiat A54 with 135 HP, Siemens Halske 11 with 100 HP, and Walter Venus with 110 HP.
