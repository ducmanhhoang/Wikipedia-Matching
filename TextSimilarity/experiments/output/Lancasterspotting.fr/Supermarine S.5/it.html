6Its extremely aerodynamic had long been studied in the wind tunnels of the Royal Aircraft Establishment and the National Physical Laboratory.
7The result led to a highly streamlined, with a surface maximum frontal aircraft only 0,548 m2.The engine was the 12-cylinder W Napier Lion VII engine, liquid cooled, which also developed the power of 875 hp @ 3 300 rpm/min.
