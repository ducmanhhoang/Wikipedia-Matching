0DescrizioneÈ a small perennial plant, a parasite of Cistaceae such as Cistus salviifolius, on whose roots it grows normally.
1Does not exceed 7 cm in height, the stem is short and covered with fleshy scales.
2The leaves are also fleshy, brightly coloured yellow and have a peak that tends to orange and to red.
3The flowers are clustered on a spike; the female ones are placed at the bottom, the masculine ones at the top.
4Are regular in shape and with perianth yellow four teeth.
5The fruit is yellow and is a sort of berry where the coating is derived from the perianth (acrosarco).
6The seeds are immersed in a pulp sticky.
7The flowering period is from April to may.
8Distribution and habitatÈ widespread throughout the mediterranean region and in Macaronesia.
9In Italy it is widespread in the centre-south, but were also found in the Lombardy region.
10Habitats are the maquis and the garrigue, which grows on the roots of the Cistacee.
